Example workflows

Medication risk missed in a draft summary

A patient summary omits a medication interaction. Without Sigrun, it ships. With Sigrun:

  1. Sigrun checks the draft against source records for missing safety risks, required structure, and unsupported claims.
  2. It runs a second verifier pass and assigns confidence to the result.
  3. If confidence is low or a critical check fails, release is blocked and routed to human review — with every input, check, and decision logged and replayable.

911 call closed before intake is complete

A caller reports a crime, but key intake is skipped and the call ends early. Without Sigrun, that failure can pass unnoticed. With Sigrun:

  1. Sigrun enforces required call-flow steps before topic shifts, transfer, or hang-up are allowed.
  2. It verifies that key fields (like caller identity for reportable incidents) are captured and that promised transfer actions actually happen.
  3. If a rule is missed or confidence drops, it blocks closure and escalates to a human dispatcher — with every turn, check result, and decision logged and replayable.
