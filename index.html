AI-Proposed Refund Under Policy Control

  1. AI proposes a refund of $6,200.
  2. Policy v3.2 high-stakes rule: refunds above $5,000 require staged approval (manager → VP) within 48 hours.
  3. Manager reviews first; if approved, VP is notified next.
  4. Refund is issued only after required stage approvals are complete.
  5. All inputs, decisions, approvers, and policy version are logged and replayable.
Edge-case handling

If manager approval times out, the request escalates automatically to VP.

If VP approval times out, the refund is rejected and finance/risk is notified.
