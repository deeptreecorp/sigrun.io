AI-Generated Medical Summary Under Verification Control

  1. AI drafts a patient summary from call transcript + chart notes.
  2. Policy v3.2 high-stakes rule: required checks include grounding, formatting, contraindication mentions, and SOP adherence.
  3. A second verifier pass scores confidence and flags unsupported claims.
  4. If confidence is below threshold, output is routed to human review before release.
  5. All inputs, checks, reviewer decisions, and policy version are logged and replayable.
Edge-case handling

If review times out, escalation routes to on-call clinical ops.

If critical check fails, output is blocked and incident review is triggered.
